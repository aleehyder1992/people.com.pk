STRATEGY AND CONSULTING

We at People™ develop HR strategies and organizational
designs to ensure harmony across the entire organizational
network.

We do not only propose solutions but fully engage in its
implementation, with our core focus being the actual
establishment of business divisions and functions in a
wholesome manner. This involves analyzing our clients’
business, capital, systematic and HR requirements to
understand their overall business philosophy in relation to
market realities and trends.

  • Uniform & Equipment
  • Registrations with all Statutory bodies
  • Statutory payments (EOBI, SS, NI etc.) and Income tax
  • Managing any legal disputes of the outsourced resources.
  • Payroll management
