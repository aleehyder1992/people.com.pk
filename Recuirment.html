RECUIRMENT


Over the past 20 years, People has built an extensive and far-
reaching global search network that allows us to offer the very
best candidates to meet our clients’ recruitment needs. We
engage in recruitment assignments ranging from hiring of
individuals for managerial or executive positions, as well as
large-scale bulk recruitment of up to hundreds of employees
at a time.


People has recruited and outplaced more than 100,000
employees worldwide. Clients around the globe trust and
employ People’s expert services to relieve themselves of the
monotonous processes of recruitment, allowing them to focus
their time and resources on more strategic areas of their
business.


Scope of Services under Recruitment includes:

  • Sourcing of Candidates through internal search network
  • Providing clients with Access to People's candidate database
  • Advertising Positions of various platforms
  • Conducting Preliminary Interviews
  • Liason and Arrangement of Final Interviews with Client
  • Free of Cost Replacement
  • Providing Guidance regarding visa Application to International Candidates
  • Evaluation of Skills through Trade Testing Centers
