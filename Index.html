Our Story

The company was initially established on 1st July 2001 in
Karachi, Pakistan under the name of Equity Exchange & Trust (EET).People began consulting in the area of
non- conventional structured finance and HR management. Its
outsourcing arm was established in 2002 under the name of
Professional Employer Services. Later in 2005, the Executive
Search division was established, specialising in service delivery
for recruitment, pre-screening, selection and staff placement
across Pakistan. The Physical Verification division was
established in the same year.

In 2007, Professional Employer Services was converted to
Professional Employers Private Limited (People™). In 2008, the
consulting, verification and executive search divisions of EET
were transferred to People™ and an international office was
inaugurated in Dubai. EET now operates as a holding
company for People™ and other group interests in luxury real
estate, hospitality and agriculture. Today, People™ is the
leading staffing solutions, process outsourcing and HR service
provider across the Middle East and South-Central Asia.​

As of June 2018, People™ is officially operating in London
