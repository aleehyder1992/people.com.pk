EMPLOYEE OUTSOURCING

Businesses today are keenly focusing on ways to relieve their
managers from performing tasks which are repetitive, routine
and add little value to their profit objectives. Over the past few
decades, employee outsourcing has emerged as the solution
to this problem and People™ has been at the forefront of this
transition. We present our clients with a unique model,
seamlessly amalgamating several HR processes to produce a
comprehensive staffing solution.


By outsourcing their staff to People™, Clients are effectively
relieved from employees’ day to day HR operations, saving
them valuable time and enabling them to focus on more
strategic and value adding initiatives.


The scope of work under Staffing Solutions Includes:

  • Hiring & Replacement
  • Medical and Group Life Insurance Management
  • Uniform & Equipment
  • Registrations with all Statutory bodies
  • Statutory payments (EOBI, SS, NI etc.) and Income tax
  • Managing any legal disputes of the outsourced resources.
  • Payroll management

How Does Employee Outsourcing Work

  • The Client signs an Employee Outsourcing agreement with PeopleTM
  • The Client’s staff receives a termination letter from the Client along with an Employment Letter from PeopleTM on the same day!
  • Clients employees become PeopleTM’s employees on paper, but still work for the client
